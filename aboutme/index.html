Adam Danial Ingah (born July 3, 2003), is an American gazillionaire[citation needed] and overall great guy.[citation needed] I don't feel like writing the rest of this article in third person, so I won't because it's my page and I can do whatever I want!!!

Anyway, I am a graduate student at the University of Washington pursuing my M.S. in Electrical Engineering. I have many interests and hobbies that I can't even begin to list them all here. I try to expose myself to everything life has to offer.

OK. Try and catch up on my lore. It's not that interesting. I was born in Virginia, right outside D.C., but I grew up in Aurora, Colorado (pretty much Denver). Of all the places on Earth, that city definitely feels the most like "home" to me. I've lived in Seattle for the past year after spending four years in Alabama. It seems like the south never fully recovered from losing the Civil War,[citation needed] so I am glad to end up in the Pacific Northwest.

One question I get a lot is: "Where are you from?". Most people think I'm Filipino, which is sort of true. My mom is from Malaysia, and my dad is from Indonesia. I'm not sure what that makes me, but I tell people one or the other if they really pry. Ethnically I am Southeast Asian. But really in my heart I believe that I have always been and always will be an American.

One weird thing though – I took a DNA test once and it came back from all sorts of places, one of which was Filipino (13%). I kept checking every other year and that percentage kept increasing by 1%.[citation needed] Whatever the hell that means. The company that did the test went bankrupt, so the Filipino dwelling within me has been growing unchecked for the past few years. I guess the most accurate thing to say is "Austronesian". At the end of the day, all the islander/archipelago guys and gals are more alike than they are different. Don't kill me for saying that. CLICK ME TO REVEAL THE TRUTH ABOUT MY ETHNICITY

What else is there to know? I have three sisters. Two older, one younger. We're close-ish (2–4 year age gap) in age. Zero brothers. My favorite color is green. I dislike receiving birthday presents.
